PlayGround

Sport is a big phenomenon, international by nature, and the focus of a diverse cultural attention. Sport brings people together, helps develop bonds, improve teamwork and the overall production of happiness amongst people. However, finding the right people, places and gear to work with is not always an easy task, especially if you are the new person in town; well PlayGround is here to do all that work for you while you lay back and just click a few times. PlayGround is a website that allows people interested in playing their favourite sport find others with the same desires, create a team and look for the available courses, places and specific gear in order to make the playing experience as easy and nice as possible. PlayGround introduces a special user rating that enables players give a quantical qualification to each team member based on punctuality, behaviour and skill so that no surprises will be encountered once all the matches are ready to be played. There are no excuses anymore, the whole city is your PlayGround!
